数据变量的分类

  • 按照取值类型：分类数据（Categorical）和数值数据 (Numerical)
  • 按照测量水平： 定类 (Nominal Scale)、定序 (Ordinal Scale)、定距 (Interval Scale)、定比例 (Ratio Scale)
  • 按照获取时间：截面数据（Cross-sectional Data）、面板数据（Panel Data）, 时间序列数据（Time-Series Data)

分类数据（Categorical）和数值数据 (Numerical)

定类 (Nominal Scale)、定序 (Ordinal Scale)、定距 (Interval Scale)、定比例 (Ratio Scale)

Categorical variables, sometimes called nominal variables, are similar to ordinal variables except that the responses are unordered. For example,种族、血型、国家和地区、性别

举例子: Nominal Scale

  What is your gender?  M-Male/F-Female
  Where do you live? 1-Surburbs/2-city/3-Town

Ordianl Scale that have ordered values such as self-reported health status with respone options ‘’Excellent, Very Good, Good, Fair Poor.’’ While the response options are ordered, they are not necessarily equally spaced.

举例子：Ordinal Scale

  - Ranking of school students? -1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.

  - Rating of restaurants
  
  - the frequency of occurrences 描述事情发生的频率 -very often, often, not often, not at all
  
  - the degree of agreement 同意程度 - totaly agree完全同意、agree同意、nuetral中立、disagree不同意、totally disagree完全不同意

Interval Scale, also called an integer, is defined as a data type which is measured along a scale, in which each point is placed at equal distance from one another.

举例子：Interval Scale

  - situations where attitude scales are considered to be interval scales
  - temeprature, family income
  - Likert Scale 
  - Semential Differential

Ratio Scale, is defined as a variable measurement scale that not only produces the order of variables but also makes the difference between variables known along with information on the value of true zero.

举例子：Ratio Scale

  - 身高
  - 体重

更直观一些：定类 (Nominal)、定序 (Ordinal)、定距 (Interval)、定比例 (Ratio)之间的关系

LEVELS OF MEASUREMENT

总结：

  • 名义或类别变量，其具体数值只是代表一个类别的标签
    对于名义或者类别变量的分析通常使用百分比（percentile）或者众数(mode)

  • ordinal强调order而非difference 使用ordinal sacle来描述non-mathematical ideas such as frequency, satisfaction, happiness, a degree of pain等

  • intervals包含ordinal scale所有的一切性质，并且还能使得不同变量之间做差值，且相同差值之间是相等的。

为什么我们要强调types of measurement scale?

在SPSS中，import数据之后，要在variable view中进行相应的“类型”和“测量”的设置

截面数据（Cross-sectional Data）、时间序列数据（Time-Series Data)、面板数据（Panel Data）

截面数据（Cross-sectional Data）: 在同一时间（时期或时点）截面上反映一个总体的一批（或全部）个体的同一特征变量的观测值，是样本数据中的常见类型之一。

举例子

  - 同一时间点所获取的，不同游客的消费偏好、旅行规划行为等
  - 同一时间点所获取的，不同学生在同一门学科上的学习表现
  - 同一时间点所获取的，不同员工在工作单位的绩效和满意度等

More information about Corss sectional data or corss sectional study, pls refer What is Cross Sectional Study: Free Example & Types

时间序列数据（Time-Series Data) ：在不同时间上收集到的数据，用于所描述现象随时间变化的情况。这类数据反映了某一事物、现象等随时间的变化状态或程度。

举例子

  - 旅客人数
  - 旅客消费指数
  - 汇率
